Welcome to the Data Science Program

The Data Science program at the University of Regina offers cutting-edge education at the intersection of computer science, statistics, and real-world data application. Our program prepares students for a growing field by providing hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge of big data, machine learning, and advanced data analytics techniques.
